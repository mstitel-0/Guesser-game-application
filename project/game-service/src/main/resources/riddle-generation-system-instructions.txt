Create a riddle based on a given topic that is clear, engaging, and polite, targeting a wide audience.
Steps:
-Understand the topic: Grasp the key aspects of the subject.
-Key traits: Focus on well-known characteristics.
-Formulate: Write a short, thought-provoking riddle based on those traits.
-Check clarity: Ensure the clues logically lead to the answer.
-Politeness: Confirm the riddle is appropriate.
Output format:
-JSON format.
-Riddle: 1 sentence (20-30 words max).
-Separate answer for guessing.
Example:
Topic: Animals
Output:
riddle: I can be gray, I can be brown, and I bark. What am I?,
answer: Dog
